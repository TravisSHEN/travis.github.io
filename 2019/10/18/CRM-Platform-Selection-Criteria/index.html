CRM Platform Selection Criteria
Post：2019-10-18 17:44:43 Tags：/ CRM / Salesforce / Dynamics365 / Visit：

This will be a great opportunity to put down a record on the journey of selecting a CRM platform with identified criteria.

CRM Platform Selection Criteria

During the very period of analyzing CRM platforms, a thourough list of criteria has been drawn out in terms of platform strategy, platform features, non-functional requirements, partnership ecosystem and other criteria.

Product strategy

The strategy of the product would take a significant role in determination for the choice, as the alignment between the growth of an organization and the product roadmap will also decide the strategy of applying such product.

Roadmap

This criterion determines if the product will be suitable for an organization along with a certain period of time.

For example, Salesforce, the leading CRM platform, will invest and put efforts on AI, the Einstein, in near future, which can be sale point for those organizations who want to take the advantage of AI.

Localization

This determines not only the ffeatures but also the efforts while selling a product to certain area.

China, who has strict laws on data security, will require the product to meet certain criterias in terms of data storage, transmission, and exposure while utilizing the product locally. Therefore, different company may take different approaches to meet such law, while deploying in China. Dynamics365 from Microsoft cuts off few parts of it features, meanwhile, Salesforce anounce a strategy partnership with AliCloud to migrate its whole platform into China physically.

Globalization

This matters to the commpany that will roll out a single set of solution to its affiliates located globally over the world. The availability and level of support across multiple areas will be important in this case.

Product features

Features, a service provider can offer, would be the key role for an organization to make the choice. Usually, a CRM platform can provide services in the areas of sales, marketing, and customer service. In recent years, CRM providers invest their efforts on AI in order to promote the effectiveness and efficiency in the area of lead management, marketing automation, and so on.

Non-functional requirements

System Performance

API Performance

Provided APIs should bear with high performance in terms of Networking IO, response time with respects to requirements of business.

Response time

Users should be able to see the response from the page within 10s.

Data Security

Security & Confidentiality

The product should guarentee to protect data from intentional or accidental destruction, modification or disclosure.

Access control

The product should have fine control over access, which also required to log user operations over data.

System architecture

The product should have clear and nice system architecture, which conform to industry’s best practice.

Integration

System integration

Ability to integrate with ESB, EAI, EIM, BPEL, Social Applications and so on.

Data integration

Ability to integrate different types of data sources.

Flexibility

The product shipped with ranges of APIs to allow customization. The product should also come with configuration first design.

Open

Standards APIs and protocols shipped with the product.

Configurable and customizable

The level of configurable and customizable.

Configurable business flows and rules

The product should provide easy-to-use UI to manage and customize flows and rules to meet business requirements

Extendability

The product should be extendable in terms of data model, module reusability, cloud-first and so on. In addition, the product should come with development ecosystem that allows developer easily build up environment for development, testing and deployment.

Usability

High stability and usability complies to SLA (Support level Agreement) standards.

Maintainability

Ability to monitor, diagnose, recover or upgrade. The system should ship with a mature maintenance and operation modules.

Depoyment of the product

Ability to provide services to certain areas with flexibility and language support.

User Experience (UI/UX)

Easy-to-use UI and great UX can significantly reduces resistancy while adopting users to the new platform.

Patnership Ecosystem

Partner Resouces

The provider should come with ranges of partner to provide service in implementation and maitenance locally and globally.

Success cases

The product should’ve delivered certain number of success cases across related industries.

Others

CRM Product Ranking

The product should be in leading position in great impact rankings, such as Gartner or IDC.

Legit

The product should be legit in terms of data and operation.

After-sales support

The provider should provide good support in terms change management, SLA, and issue management.
