2020-01-08 Market Segmentation
Post：2020-01-09 12:15:10 Tags：/ marketing / IMC / Visit：

As there is no way to establish a single market strategy for every consumers, the marketer attemps to identify broad classes of buyer, which divide up markets into distinct groups,

Segmentation Approaches

Geographic Segmentation

Markets are divided into different geographic units, incl. nations, states, counties, or even neighborhoods.

Demographic Segmentation

Dividing the markets based on age, sex, family size, education, income, and social class.

Psychographic Segmentation

Dividing the markets based on personality and/or lifestyles.

Tip: lifestyle has been mostly considred as the most effective criterion for segmentation.

Determination of lifestyle is based on an analysis of the activities, interests, and opinions (AIO) of consumers.

Behavioristic Segmentation

Dividing consumers into groups based on their usage, loyalties, or buying responses to a product.

Benefit Segmentation

Dividing consumers into groups based on their specific needs and/or wants.

E.g. considering the purchase of wristwatch, a diver will put water resistance as a criterion over stylishness.
